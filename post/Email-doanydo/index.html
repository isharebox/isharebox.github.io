Emailing tasks to your personal lists can easily be done by simply sending an email to do@any.do using the email address associated with your Any.do account (as shown under Settings - Profile in your Any.do app).

Once the email is sent, a new Task will be created in Any.do under your default list. To make sure that the tasks are added properly, please make sure to follow these guidelines:

  • The email must have a subject ("Re" and "FWD" are not considered a subject).

    • The subject of the email will become the task's title.

  • The email has to be written in text and not in Html format.

    • The content of the email will be added in the notes section of the task.
